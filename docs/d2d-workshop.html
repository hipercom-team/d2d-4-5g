Indo-French Workshop on D2D Communications for 5G and IoT Networks


The partners of the CEFIPRA indo-french project "D2D for 5G" (IISc, IITK, Inria, Nokia, Telecom ParisTech, TIFR) propose a Workshop on D2D Communications for 5G and Internet of Things Networks, organized by Telecom ParisTech and Nokia. Presentations address the state of the art in D2D in the context of both 5G and IoT networks as well as recent scientific results (see program below).

Program:
  • 9:00-9:45: Café et accueil
  • 9:45-10:00: Vinod KUMAR (Nokia), Amira ALLOUM (Nokia), Marceau COUPECHOUX (TPT) Ouverture
  • 10:00-11:00: Tutoriel : An overwiew of D2D in 3GPP LTE standard, Sofia MARTINEZ LOPEZ (Orange Labs)
  • 11:00-11:30: Pause café
  • 11:30-12:30: Plénière : Connectionless Access for Green Wireless Networks, Philippe JACQUET (Nokia)
  • 12:30-02:00: déjeuner
  • 02:00-02:30: Resource Allocation Schemes for D2D Communication, Mohamad ASSAAD (CentraleSupélec)
  • 02:30-03:00: Information-Theoretic Results on D2D Networks, Michèle WIGGER (TPT)
  • 03:00-03:30: Stochastic Algorithms for D2D Communications, Ketan RAJAWAT (IITK)
  • 03:30-04:00: Pause café
  • 04:00-04:30: Opportunistic Link Activation and Routing Protocols for Multi-Hop D2D Communication, Chandra MURTHY (IISc)
  • 04:30-05:00: D2D LTE and IoT, Cédric ADJIH (INRIA)
  • 05:00-05:30: Graph Matching and D2D Communication, Rahul VAZE (TIFR Bombay)
